PRIVACY POLICY - HAPPY TAPPY

Effective Date: October 6, 2025
Last Updated: October 6, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how XVS ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game "Happy Tappy" (the "Game").

Happy Tappy is designed for children aged 0-12 years and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do NOT collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses from players
  • We do NOT collect photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • We do NOT collect precise location data (GPS)
  • We do NOT use behavioral advertising or user profiling

2.2 Information We May Collect

  • Game Progress Data: Saved locally on your device only (scores, unlocked levels, settings)
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility and crash prevention)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous error logs to improve game stability
  • Purchase Information: Transaction records for in-app purchases (processed securely by Google Play)
  • Advertising Identifier: Anonymous device advertising ID (for non-personalized ads only)

Important: All data is collected and processed in compliance with COPPA. We treat all users as children under 13.

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the limited information we collect to: - Provide and improve the gaming experience - Display age-appropriate, non-personalized advertisements - Process in-app purchases securely - Fix bugs and improve game performance - Ensure COPPA compliance across all services - Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT use data for user profiling, behavioral advertising, or personalized content.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

4.1 Unity LevelPlay (IronSource) - Advertising & Mediation

Happy Tappy displays advertisements through Unity LevelPlay (powered by IronSource), which may collect: - Device advertising identifier (non-persistent) - Device model and operating system version - IP address (used only for general location at city level) - Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

COPPA Compliance Measures: - All ads are marked as child-directed (is_child_directed=true) - Device ID collection is opt-out (is_deviceid_optout=true) - Only age-appropriate content is displayed - NO personalized advertising or behavioral tracking - NO collection of persistent identifiers from children

Ad Networks integrated through LevelPlay: Unity LevelPlay may serve ads from multiple child-safe networks including: - Unity Ads - Google AdMob (via mediation) - AppLovin MAX (via mediation)

All ad networks are configured for COPPA compliance and child-directed treatment.

Unity LevelPlay Privacy Policy: https://www.is.com/privacy-policy/
IronSource COPPA Compliance: https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/general/coppa-compliance/

4.2 Google Play In-App Billing

For in-app purchases (e.g., "No Ads" option), we use Google Play's secure billing system: - Google processes all payment information securely - We do NOT store payment details on our servers or devices - Purchase records are managed by Google Play - Parents control purchase permissions through Google Play Family settings

Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 Unity Analytics (Limited)

We may use Unity Analytics for basic app performance metrics only: - Session duration and frequency (anonymous) - Device specifications (model, OS version) - Crash reports (no personal data) - General app usage patterns (anonymous)

COPPA Compliance: Unity Analytics is configured for child-directed treatment with limited data collection.

Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do NOT sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties except: - Service Providers: Unity LevelPlay, Google Play (under strict COPPA-compliant agreements) - Legal Requirements: As required by law, court order, or governmental regulation - Safety Protection: To protect the rights, safety, or security of our users - Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with continued privacy protection)

Important: All third-party services used by Happy Tappy are vetted for COPPA compliance.

6. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures: - Local Storage: Game progress stored locally on device (encrypted by Android/iOS) - Encrypted Transmission: All server communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption - Limited Data Collection: Minimal data collection principle (only what's necessary) - Access Controls: Restricted access to any collected data - Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA COMPLIANCE)

Special Protection for Children Under 13:

Happy Tappy treats ALL users as children under 13 and implements strict COPPA protections:

  • ✅ NO personal information collection from children
  • ✅ NO behavioral advertising or personalized ads
  • ✅ Age-appropriate content only (rated for ages 0-12)
  • ✅ Parental controls supported through device/Google Play settings
  • ✅ Child-directed treatment enabled across all ad networks
  • ✅ COPPA-compliant third-party services only

Parental Rights Under COPPA:

Parents have the right to: 1. Review any information collected from their child (contact us at email below) 2. Request deletion of their child's information 3. Refuse further collection of their child's information 4. Control in-app purchases through Google Play Family settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: daneneoficial@gmail.com

8. YOUR CHOICES AND CONTROLS

8.1 Advertising Options

  • Remove All Ads: Purchase the "No Ads" in-app option to completely disable all advertising
  • Ad Settings: Ads are NOT personalized for children (COPPA requirement)
  • Ad Frequency: Limited ad display frequency for child-friendly experience

8.2 Data Management

  • Local Data: Delete the app to remove all locally stored game progress
  • Advertising ID: Reset advertising ID through device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads; iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising)
  • Restore Purchases: Purchase history maintained securely by Google Play

8.3 Parental Controls

  • In-App Purchases: Control through Google Play Family settings or device restrictions
  • App Deletion: Permanently removes all local data from device

9. DATA RETENTION

  • Game Progress: Stored locally on device until app is deleted by user
  • Purchase Records: Maintained by Google Play per their retention policies
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained only for service improvement (maximum 24 months)
  • Advertising Data: Managed by Unity LevelPlay per their retention policies (temporary, non-persistent)
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes, then deleted

Data Deletion: To request deletion of any data, contact us at daneneoficial@gmail.com

10. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place: - GDPR Compliance: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers - COPPA Compliance: All international transfers maintain child privacy protections - Secure Transmission: Encrypted data transfer protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
