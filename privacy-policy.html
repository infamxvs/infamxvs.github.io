Privacy Policy

App: Easy Guitar Tuner & Metronome
Developer: XVS
Last updated: 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Easy Guitar Tuner & Metronome (the “App”) handles your information. We respect your privacy and have designed the App to work without collecting personal data.

Summary

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It does not require an account, and it does not contain tracking or analytics that identify you.

Microphone access

The App uses your device microphone solely to detect the pitch of the sound being played, in real time, so it can show the musical note and tuning on screen. Audio is processed locally on your device only. Audio is never recorded, never stored, and never transmitted to us or to any third party.

Internet access

The App connects to the internet for a single purpose: on startup it downloads a small public file to check whether a newer version of the App is available. This request does not include any personal data about you.

Data stored on your device

The App may write a local diagnostic log file in its private application storage on your device. This file stays on your device, is overwritten on each launch, and is not sent anywhere.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data, because the App does not collect personal data.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If the App's functionality changes (for example, if advertising is introduced in a future version), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and the “Last updated” date above will change.
