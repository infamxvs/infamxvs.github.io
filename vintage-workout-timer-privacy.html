Privacy Policy - Vintage Workout Timer

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vintage Workout Timer! This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it. Vintage Workout Timer is a retro-themed workout and interval timer (with workout programs, a stopwatch and a countdown timer) for Android devices.

We respect your privacy. The app was designed to work without any user account and to keep your data on your own device. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

In short: Vintage Workout Timer does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and stores your workout programs and timer settings only on your device. The only data leaving your device is what is collected by Google's advertising service (AdMob) to display ads.

2. Information stored on your device

The app saves the following data locally on your device only (using Android's local storage). This information is never uploaded to us or to any server:

  • Workout programs: the workout plans you create or edit (exercise names, durations, groups, repetitions).
  • Timer presets: the countdown timer values you configure.

Because this data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app removes it. We have no access to it and no copy of it.

3. Information collected by third parties (Advertising)

3.1 Google AdMob

Vintage Workout Timer is free and displays a banner advertisement using Google AdMob (a service provided by Google LLC / Google Ireland Limited). To serve ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Device and advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID).
  • Approximate, non-precise information derived from your IP address (for example, general region) and basic device/usage information used to serve and measure ads.

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. For details on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, see: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

3.2 Consent (GDPR / UMP)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, the app shows a consent form (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) the first time you open it. You can choose how your data is used for advertising. Your choice is respected by the advertising SDK.

4. Internet connection & permissions

The app uses an internet connection for two purposes only:

  • Advertising: loading the banner ad (see section 3).
  • Update check: at startup the app downloads a small public file to check whether a newer version is available. This request does not send any personal data about you.

The app requests the following Android permissions, used strictly for its core features:

  • Internet: for the two purposes above.
  • Foreground service / notifications / wake lock: so a running timer keeps counting and can continue (with sound) while the screen is off, showing a notification with PAUSE/RESUME/STOP controls.

5. Information we do NOT collect

For full transparency, the app does not collect, request or access any of the following:

  • Your name, email address or any account information (there is no sign-up).
  • Precise location (GPS) or your address.
  • Contacts, photos, files or media on your device.
  • Camera or microphone.
  • Payment information (the app has no in-app purchases).

6. Children's privacy

Vintage Workout Timer is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the advertising features, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR you have rights regarding personal data, including the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and data portability. Because the app stores your workout data only on your device (and we hold no copy of it), you can exercise the most direct form of these rights at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

For data processed by Google AdMob for advertising, you can manage your consent through the in-app consent options and through your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads), and you may also exercise your rights directly with Google. For any privacy question regarding this app, you can contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or for legal/operational reasons. Changes become effective once published on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
